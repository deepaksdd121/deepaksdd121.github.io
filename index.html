About
It gives us great pleasure to introduce HOTEL KRISHNA PALACE, Karol Bagh, New Delhi as the best Hotel in budget category with luxurious Accommodation.

The hotel is situated at the heart of Karol Bagh area oferring you lots of choices in terms of food, travel and shopping.
It is well connected to places such as The Airport (15 kms away), New Delhi Railway Station (4 kms) and Connaught Place (3 kms)

All our rooms offer a special touch of luxury to our Guests with its well furnished modern day amenities, 32 Inches Wall Mounted LED TV, Air Conditioners and other exciting facilities.
